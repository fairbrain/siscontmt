About Siscon TMT

Building India's future with premium quality TMT bars for over 25 years

Welcome to SISCON – Shivam Iron & Steel Company Ltd., one of Eastern India’s most trusted and progressive names in the iron & steel sector. An ISO 9001:2015 certified company, SISCON has been shaping the future of infrastructure since 1996 with its state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities located at Giridih and Koderma (Jharkhand).

Under the flagship brand SISCON TMT, we manufacture ISI-certified MS Steel and Thermo-Mechanically Treated (TMT) bars that stand as benchmarks of quality, strength, and innovation. Our plants are equipped with advanced, integrated technologies and in-house testing laboratories, ensuring that every product meets and exceeds global standards of durability, toughness, and ductility.

At SISCON, we take immense pride in being the backbone of India’s growth story. From bridges and highways to homes and skyscrapers, our products are at the core of iconic projects across the nation. Our flagship SISCON 800 HSD TMT bars are designed with the latest thermo-mechanical treatment technology, combining tempered martensite on the surface with fine-grain ferrite-pearlite at the core—delivering superior strength, resilience, and long-lasting performance.

Beyond products, we are deeply committed to sustainability and responsibility. Our eco-friendly operations and energy-efficient processes reflect our dedication to building a greener tomorrow while delivering unmatched value to our customers.

For over two decades, builders, architects, and engineers have trusted SISCON TMT for uncompromising quality, consistency, and reliability. Guided by our core values — excellence, sustainability, and customer-centricity — we strive to deliver not just steel, but strength that defines the future.

With a legacy of innovation and trust, SISCON stands as Eastern India’s leading TMT manufacturer, forging a stronger, safer, and more sustainable nation.

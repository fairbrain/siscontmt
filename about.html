About Siscon TMT

Building India's future with premium quality TMT bars for over 25 years

Welcome to SISCO, Shivam Iron & Steel Company Ltd, which is well known Business House in the iron & steel sector in Eastern India and a certifi ed ISO-9001:2015 Company. SISCO, has manufacturing plant at Giridih and Koderma (Jharkhand) producing ISI Certified MS Steel & TMT Bars under the Brand name of SISCON TMT With decades of expertise and innovation, SISCO is India’s premier manufacturer of high-quality MS Steel and TMT bars committed to setting benchmarks in the steel industry. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, equipped with the latest integrated technology, ensure that we deliver world-class products that meet and exceed international quality standards.

At SISCO, we take pride in being the backbone of India's infrastructure growth. Our TMT bars are trusted by builders, architects, and engineers for their superior strength, durability, and resilience. From bridges and highways to homes and skyscrapers, our products are integral to projects that shape the future. Guided by our core values of excellence, sustainability, and customer-centricity, we focus on delivering not just products but value. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices and energy-efficient processes reflects our dedication to a greener tomorrow

SISCO, stands as the leading TMT manufacturer in Eastern India and known for delivering unmatched quality and reliability. With a deep-rooted commitment to excellence, we have become a trusted name in the construction and infrastructure sectors. Choose SISCON TMT, a product where innovation meets trust, and strength builds the future. Together, let’s forge a stronger India with – MAJBOOTI KI NAYI PEHCHAAN

Why Choose SISCON TMT?

Superior Values of Strength and High Ductility

SISCON TMX bars show rare combination of high strength with excellent ductility. The tensile to yield strength ratios are always greater than 1.15.

Superior Corrosion Resistance

Lower carbon content and the absense of torsional residual stress allows better corrosion re This unique property of SISCON TMX bars is further enhanced through a martensite laye outside surface, uniform microstructure and an adherent scale film formed by water quenchi rolling.

Excellent Bendability

The tough outer layer of martensite and the ductile core of the SISCON TMX bars results in bendability. This Ferrite Pearlite Structure allows these bars to be bent with ease.

Superior Rib Pattern

SISCON TMX bars have unique rib patterns resulting in formation of a strong bond with concrete. The mean projected rib of SISCON TMX is more than the specified values of the IS: 1786 standard. The uniformity of the rib pattern ensures uniform strong bonding with concrete for the entire structure.

Seismic Property

With superior seismic properties, SISCON TMX bars ensure better protection and minimum damage to the structure in the event of an earthquake. Additional area under the curve shows the extra energy than can be dissipated before breaking. High UTS/YS indicates high strain hardening rate beyond yeild point-a requirement for EQR steel International standards also require a minimum uniform elongation at maximum stress.
